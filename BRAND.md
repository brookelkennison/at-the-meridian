# At The Meridian — Brand Kit

Dark, blue-tinted editorial aesthetic. Condensed industrial headlines, humanist sans body, sky-blue accent.

## Backgrounds
- Base background: `#0a0c14`
- Alt / elevated card: `#11141d`
- Deeper card / inset: `#181c28`

## Text (foreground)
- Primary text: `#f5f7fb`
- Dimmed text (72%): `rgba(245, 247, 251, 0.72)`
- Faint text (42%): `rgba(245, 247, 251, 0.42)`

## Lines / borders
- Subtle line: `rgba(190, 210, 240, 0.10)`
- Strong line: `rgba(190, 210, 240, 0.22)`

## Accent (sky blue — signature)
- Accent: `#7dd3fc`
- Accent bright: `#bae6fd`
- Accent deep: `#0369a1`
- Text on accent: `#0a0c14`

## Secondary spot accents (use sparingly)
- Blue: `#0d99ff`
- Cyan: `#61fdfc`
- Pink: `#ff24bd`
- Purple: `#9e36ff`
- Yellow: `#ffcd29`
- Red: `#f24822`

## Typography
- Headlines / logo / eyebrows: **Norwester** (condensed industrial caps)
- Body & paragraphs: **Fustat** (humanist sans)
- Mono / labels: **Space Grotesk**
- Display headings: tight tracking (-0.02 to -0.03em), line-height ~0.92–1.0
